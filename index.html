About

The game will be based on the original VPet pendant series, that means:

  • You will start with an egg
  • After 5 minutes or a minimum of love it will hatch
  • All first stage Digis are Botamon (all Botamon are the same at hatching)
  • All second second stage Digis are Koromon, but their stats will be influenced by the care you give your Botamon

From there you will be given a Digi based on the care that you give them!

Future versions will have options for battles and possibly trading/breeding.
